Chang the baboon

Once upon a time there was a baboon named Chang. He was a very clever ape. He loved stealing food from humans. One day mzee Kokoo saw that his potatoes and carrots were missing. He held a meeting with Simba the lion king. All the smartest animals including Chang were called to give advice and have a fair trial. They sugested that they ask every one of the animals if they did it. But Chang himself sugested that they have a trap set if the thief comes again. Chang had a plan to eliminate the threat of anyone catching him stealing. He was one of the people to set the trap for and he was their leader because he was the most agile and smartest of the team. He lied to the hyena that he was there to test the trap and whatever way it goes he gets food. At midnight the hyena went and got caught in the trap and wrestled with Dodo the mans daughter. The hyena was exiled from the land and the old man was at peace. A week later the old man died and his paranoid son inherited his land. While the man died the boy was in the room. when he left to spread the news chang made a gash in the mans chest and poured blood on it. After Chang escaped he shouted that it was the boy who killed him. The lion believed him and ordered an execution. Meanwhile his inteligent sister was colecting the data that that the camera she placed on Chang earlier. She showed the lion the proof and chand was killed in place of the boy.The moral is use your inteligence wisely dont be Evil with it
