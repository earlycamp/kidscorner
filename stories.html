Zulu the monkey

Once upon a time there lived a monkey named ZULU. Zulu loved eating bannanas and sometimes she would even go to the extent of stealing them. one day zulu decided she wanted cooked bananas and pork for lunch but she ran out of bannanas. She went to her farm to look for some bannanas but none had grown.She decided she would steal from some humans. The humans however set a trap for her.
